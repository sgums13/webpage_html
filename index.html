MC from the Bay Area, who aspires to share the Knowledge she learned growing up through her music. She performed at the Empowering Women of Color Conference in 2012 and 2014.

Her mix-tape, "Rejuvenated" was released in December, 2013 on hotnewhiphop.com and Soundcloud. She has recently been interviewed on KPFA radio in Berkeley, and featured in 4 podcasts in Virginia.
